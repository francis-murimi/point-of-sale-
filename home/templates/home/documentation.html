ANALYBIZ is record keeping system. It performs calculations and analysis of the data it receives.

We will describe it in two ways.
  • Provision of data
  • Analysis of data
PROVISION OF DATA
What data?
Identification data

This is the information you will provide for identification. You will only provide it once during registration.
It includes:

  • Personal Details
    1. Username
    2. Password
    3. Valid email address
    4. Names
  • Business Details
    1. Business name
    2. Business category
    3. Business Location
      • Town
      • County
    4. Subscription type

Operational Data

This is the data that will be used in deriving analysis and calculations

It includes:

  1. Products
    • Product name
    • Product cost
    • Stock
    • Price
    • A short product description
  2. Product Category
      Example:
    • Screens,Phones,Sound systems e.t.c for electrical shops
    • Fertilizers,Herbicides,Fungicides e.t.c for agrovets.

There are no limitations to the naming of this items. You can categorize them in any logical way you see best

You can change the names and properties of all this items at any time.

How to record sales

This process has been made simple by using real world procedures. We will use a cart system.

It works like a trolley in a supermarket. You products that you sell and and save them as an order.

Tips to use the cart:
  1. Add products purchased by one customer in the same order and save.
  2. You can add or remove products and change the quantity of products in the cart using the buttons provided.
Analysis of data

In this part, we will use two formats.

  1. All time analysis.

    This will show you information for since you created an account.

    Information shown
    1. Total sales
    2. Total cost of all items you have sold.
    3. Total profit made.
    4. The profit rate
    5. Total number of items sold for each product
  2. Select time

    For this method, you will select a single date and all the analysis will be based on that date.

    Analysis based on the select date
    • Daily analysis

      You will see:

      1. Total sales made on that day
      2. Total cost of products sold on that day
      3. Total profit made on that day
      4. Profit rate of that day
      5. All orders made on that day

    • Weekly analysis

      It will show analysis of all the past six days of the selected date

      You will see:

      1. Total sales made on that week
      2. Total cost of products sold on that week
      3. Total profit made on that week
      4. Profit rate of that week
      5. Number of product items sold for each product
      6. All orders made on that week

    • Monthly analysis

      It will show analysis of the month of the selected date

      You will see:

      1. Total sales made on that month
      2. Total cost of products sold on that month
      3. Total profit made on that month
      4. Profit rate of that month
      5. All orders made on that week

    • Yearly analysis

      It will show analysis of the year of the selected date

      You will see:

      1. Total sales made on that year
      2. Total cost of products sold on that year
      3. Total profit made on that year
      4. Profit rate of that year
      5. Number of product items sold for each product
      6. All orders made on that year

With the analysis provided, we will post ways to interpret them and use the knowledge to make better business decisions
